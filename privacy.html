Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2026

Who We Are

EmployAb is a product of Tachyon Tech Ltd, registered in Ireland. Our registered address is in Wicklow, Ireland. When we say "we", "us", or "our", we mean Tachyon Tech Ltd.

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at gdpr@employab.com.

What Data We Collect

We are currently in pre-launch. At this stage, we only collect data you voluntarily provide when you contact us, including:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your organisation and role
  • The content of your message

When our platform launches, this policy will be updated to reflect any additional data processing. We will notify registered contacts of material changes.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Share updates about EmployAb if you have opted in
  • Evaluate potential partnerships

Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Legitimate interest -responding to business enquiries
  • Consent -where you opt in to receive updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal data with third parties except where required by law or with service providers who process data on our behalf under appropriate data processing agreements.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as it is needed for the purpose it was collected. You can request deletion at any time by contacting gdpr@employab.com.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission

Cookies and Tracking

This website uses Google Tag Manager (GTM) for anonymous site analytics. GTM is only loaded after you give explicit consent via the cookie banner shown on your first visit.

If you accept cookies:

  • GTM may set cookies to help us understand how visitors use the site
  • No personal data is collected, sold, or shared with third parties
  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous

If you reject cookies, no tracking scripts are loaded and no cookies are set. Your preference is stored in your browser's local storage so you are not asked again.

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our platform develops. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
