How EmployAb works.

Four steps from setup to results. Your university gets a branded platform, your graduates get verified credentials, and employers find the talent they need.

Four Steps to Better Graduate Outcomes

1

We Set You Up

Your branded platform, designed to go live in weeks. We handle the technical integration with your existing systems — registrar, LMS, careers service. You focus on your students and employer relationships.

2

Skills Get Verified

As students complete modules and placements, their skills are automatically verified against the ESCO taxonomy and issued as Open Badges 3.0 credentials — trusted and portable across all EU member states.

3

Graduates Get Matched

Employers search your talent pool directly. Our AI matching — explainable and EU AI Act compliant — connects graduates to roles that match their verified skills, not their keyword-stuffed CVs.

4

You See the Results

Employment rates, time-to-hire, salary uplift — all in real-time dashboards. Data ready for your leadership, your funders, HEA, QQI, and your accreditors. No more waiting 12 months for survey results.
